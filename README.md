
#  Sleep & Anxiety Analysis

##  Project Overview

Sleep plays a crucial role in maintaining both physical and mental well-being. This project explores how sleep habits, anxiety levels, and lifestyle factors are related by analyzing responses collected through a Google Forms survey.

The dataset was cleaned and preprocessed using **Python (Pandas)** and then visualized using **Power BI** to create an interactive dashboard.

---

##  Objectives

* Clean and preprocess raw survey data.
* Analyze sleep patterns and anxiety levels.
* Explore the relationship between lifestyle habits and sleep quality.
* Identify commonly used non-prescription sleep solutions.
* Present findings through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

##  Dataset Information

* **Source:** Self-created Google Forms Survey
* **Responses:** 22 (after removing duplicate entries)
* **Type:** Survey Dataset
* **Features:** 20 Columns

The survey includes information about:

* Age Group
* Country
* Sleep Duration
* Sleep Difficulty
* Anxiety Frequency
* Anxiety Level
* Caffeine Intake
* Screen Time Before Bed
* Exercise Frequency
* Diet Quality
* Sleep Solutions Tried
* Side Effects
* Trust in Sleep Solutions

---

##  Key Insights

Some observations from the analysis include:

* Most respondents reported sleeping between **6–7 hours** per night.
* Anxiety levels varied across participants.
* Exercise frequency and screen time showed noticeable differences among respondents.
* Participants used a variety of non-prescription sleep solutions.
* Lifestyle factors appeared to influence perceived sleep quality.
